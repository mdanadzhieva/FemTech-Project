About Us

We are here to inspire you to get your hands dirty with all the latest advancements in IT contributed to by role-model female figures. Here we highlight some of the most newsworthy and impressive women's achievements in Technology and how these contributions have made a massive diffence to the world of IT.

Tech is changing the way we live our lives. Despite being a part of our everyday lives, technology remains inaccessible to women for various reasons. Our mission is to highlight the achievements women have made in tech despite the barriers we face.


Let's talk stats!

Did you know that according to the Women in Tech Organisation

  1. Only 1 woman in 5 people is working in the tech industry today
  2. Only 5% of leadership positions in tech are held by women

Also according to Tech Republic

  1. Women of colour occupy less than 5% of jobs in the Tech sector
  2. Employment for white people stands at 665,000 jobs, but employment for black people stands at 86,000 positions.

Our mission

We want to inspire young women to break down the barriers and get into tech. We highlight some of the most newsworthy and impressive achievements by women in technology and how these contributions have made a diffence to the world of IT. We also provide resources for women to learn how to code and get familiar with tech.
